Exploring Radioactivity

In these pages I am collecting some material about radioactivity processes and on radioactive samples you can find around you. We are surrounded by radioactive materials, so it is good to know at least a bit about it.

This is a work in progress, and the following pages have to be considered just drafts.

  • Atoms & Nuclides
    A brief introduction on what are atoms, isotopes and nuclides just to set some terminology…
  • Types of radioactive decay
    Description of the main processes which cause radioactivity.
  • Units of measure
    Radioactivity is a physical phenomenon, and, as such, we can observe it and measure it!
  • Radioactive Samples
    A small collection of radioactive materials you can find around you – or that you can easily buy.
