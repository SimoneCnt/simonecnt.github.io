
### #235 - Caliche on Sandstone from Utah, USA

**Locality:** Utah, USA  
**Size:** 8 x 5 x 5 cm  
**Weight:** 118g  

A grey caliche crust on white sandstone. Under UV, both fluoresce: the caliche
orange, the sandstone light blue. The sandstone is also briefly phosphorescent.
