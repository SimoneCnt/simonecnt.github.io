
### <a name="SC257"></a> SC257 - Schalenblende & Dolomite from the Raibl Mines, Italy

**Locality:** Raibl Mines, Cave del Predil, Tarvisio, Udine Province, Friuli Venezia Giulia, Italy ([MINDAT](https://www.mindat.org/loc-4407.html))  
**Size:** 8 x 7.5 x 3 cm  
**Weight:** 220g  
**minID:** [Q2U-7L4](https://www.mindat.org/Q2U-7L4)

Sphalerite var Schalenblende and dolomite. The sphalerite fluoresces
orange/brown while the dolomite fluoresces white; under longwave UV light.
