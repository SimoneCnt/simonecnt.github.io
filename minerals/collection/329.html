SC329 - Tetrahedrite & Sphalerite

Locality: Mundo Nuevo Mine, Mundo Nuevo, Huamachuco, Sanchez Carrion Province, La Libertad, Peru (MINDAT)
Size: 5 x 5 x 3 cm - Small cabinet (5-7 cm)
Weight: 102 g
minID: 9A1-6RM

Grey sphalerite associated with black tetrahedrite. The sphalerite fluoresces a bright orange under UV light and is triboluminescent.
